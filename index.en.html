The Story

From ruin to life's work

As an architect, I acquired this more than 125-year-old building — once part of the Son Mone country estate — in a ruinous state, with the vision of bringing it back to life according to my own ideas. The originally split house had served as accommodation for the farm workers.

My credo: preserve as much of the original as possible, to keep the character and charm of the building intact. After 37 years and several construction phases, the result is a home that combines timeless tradition with contemporary style — materials and craftsmanship of the highest standard.

The finca sits on a gently sloping plot with almond trees, just outside Andratx. Five minutes on foot to the village, 30 minutes to the airport. From the large terrace you'll experience unique sunsets over the valley and the Tramuntana mountains.

Location

Son Mone — just outside Andratx
